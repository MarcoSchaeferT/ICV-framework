#### Dengue Serotype
A non-representative data set of dengue serotypes that enables counting serotypes per country and year. (Data source: [Nextstrain](https://nextstrain.org/dengue/all/E))

> [!WARNING]
> Disclaimer: Serotype counts cannot be interpreted as dengue cases. This is not a representative data set, but rather a subset of cases in which the serotype was determined. The data comes from different laboratories that used different methods. 

> [!NOTE]
Determined serotypes with missing country information are excluded. Serotypes with missing year information are assigned to N/A in the **Year** dropdown menu. The serotype counts of the following smaller countries or oceans are currently not considered/visualized:
