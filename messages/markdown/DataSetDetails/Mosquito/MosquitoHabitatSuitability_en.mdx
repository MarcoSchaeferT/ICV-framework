#### Habitat Suitability
 The Mosquito Habitat Suitability datasets provides information on the suitability of habitats for different mosquito species. The habitat suitability values were predicted based on the [Training Data](#mosquito-training-data) and are represented in a grid format. Each grid cell (n={"267,876"}) is approximately 28km x 28km at the equator and has the following key properties:  
- 0.25° x 0.25° area
- Suitability value: 0.0-1.0 (0-100%)
- Species: ***Aedes albopictus*** or ***Aedes aegypti***
