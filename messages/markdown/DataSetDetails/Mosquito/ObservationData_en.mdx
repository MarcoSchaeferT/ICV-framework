
#### Mosquito Observation Data
The observation data is based on "The global compendium of *Aedes aegypti* and *Ae. albopictus* occurrence" published in *Scientific Data* (Article number: 150035, 2015) by Kraemer et al.

**Dataset characteristics:**
- **Time span:** 1960–2014
- **Time resolution:** yearly
- **Spatial resolution:** 5x5km – 100x100km
- **Geo-referenced records**
- **19,930** occurrences of *Ae. aegypti*
- **22,137** occurrences of *Ae. albopictus*

This represents the largest data compilation derived from published and unpublished studies on these mosquito species.
