
#### Climate Data (ECMWF ERA5)
ERA5 dataset from European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts (ECMWF) — the fifth generation ECMWF atmospheric reanalysis of the global climate. ERA5 provides hourly estimates of a large number of atmospheric, land and oceanic climate variables.

- **Spatial coverage/extent:** -180°, 90°, 180°, -90°
- **Temporal coverage:** 1940 – present
- **Spatial resolution:** 31 x 31 km
- **Temporal resolution:** 1-3 hours
