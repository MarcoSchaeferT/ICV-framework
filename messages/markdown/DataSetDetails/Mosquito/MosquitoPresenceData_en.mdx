#### Presence Data
Exact geo locations of sightings of different mosquito species worldwide for different years. The data was collected from various sources and is represented as points on the map. Each point contains the following information:  
- Latitude and Longitude
- Year of sighting
- Species: **Aedes albopictus** or **Aedes aegypti**

| Species              | Years      |
|----------------------|-------------|
| *Aedes albopictus*, *Aedes aegypti*    | 2013 - 2025   |
