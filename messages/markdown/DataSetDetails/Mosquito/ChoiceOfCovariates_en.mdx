
#### Choice of Covariates
To produce a high-resolution prediction, data with the highest spatial resolution possible is preferred. The choice of covariates is based on Lim et al. "A systematic review of the data, methods and environmental covariates used to map Aedes-borne arbovirus transmission risk" (*BMC Infect Dis* 23, 708, 2023).

**Climate variables (Monthly):**
| Variable | Resolution |
|----------|------------|
| Temperature at 2m | 0.25° x 0.25° |
| Dew-point temperature at 2m | 0.25° x 0.25° |
| Total precipitation | 0.25° x 0.25° |
| Wind speed at 10m | 0.25° x 0.25° |

**Population and land-use variables:**
| Variable | Source |
|----------|--------|
| Population (5 yearly) | GHS-POP (250 m res.) |
| Land-use (Yearly) | HILDA+ (1 km res.) |
