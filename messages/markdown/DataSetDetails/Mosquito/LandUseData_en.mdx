
##### HILDA+ Land Use/Land Cover Data
HILDA+ (HIstoric Land Dynamics Assessment+) is a global dataset on annual land use/cover and its change, based on a data-driven reconstruction approach and integrates multiple open data streams (from high-resolution remote sensing, long-term land use reconstructions and statistics).

- **Spatial coverage/extent:** -180°, 90°, 180°, -90°
- **Temporal coverage:** 1899–2019
- **Spatial resolution:** 0.01° (~1 km)
- **Temporal resolution:** 1 year

**Covers 8 categories:**
- Urban areas
- Cropland
- Pasture/rangeland
- Forest
- Unmanaged grass/shrubland
- Sparse/no vegetation
- Ocean
- Water

*Source: Winkler, Karina; Fuchs, Richard; Rounsevell, Mark D A; Herold, Martin (2020): HILDA+ Global Land Use Change between 1960 and 2019 [dataset]. PANGAEA, https://doi.org/10.1594/PANGAEA.921846*
