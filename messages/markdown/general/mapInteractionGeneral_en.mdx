### Interactions

Dynamically adjust position and zoom level to focus on your area of interest. Hover and click to further adjust and to get details on demand.

| Action    | How to Use                          |
|-----------|-------------------------------------|
| **Pan**   | Click + hold the left mouse button, then move the mouse to shift the map view. |
| **Zoom**  | Scroll the mouse wheel to zoom in or out, centered on your cursor position.      |
| **Click** | Countries are selectable. A click on country can automatically pan the view to the country center.
| **Hover** | When moving the cursor over a colored grid cells a tooltip appears, providing  detailed information on the current grid cell.

Legends in the bottom left corner show the current scale (*km per length*) and the zoom and coordinates of the center point of the view (*lat, lng, zoom*).
