This view demonstrates the probabilistic calibration and uncertainty quantification functionalities of ICV, inherited from the [Uncertainty Toolbox](https://github.com/uncertainty-toolbox/uncertainty-toolbox). Using synthetic data with random Gaussian noise, three interconnected visualizations are presented: **Temporal Uncertainty**: It visually contrasts the predicted distribution alongside the actual test data. **Probabilistic Calibration Plot**: Evaluates the model’s calibration for a grid cell, calculated across the prediction intervals of the entire test set. **Global Coefficient of Variation Map**: Displays the mapped coefficient of variation for each grid cell across the globe for one month.
