This view presents the dataset employed to train the machine learning models. The dataset underpins predictions of mosquito habitat suitability and encompasses a range of environmental variables, including climate, land use, and population density. The grid-based data are visualized on the world map (**Map View**) provided below. Individual variables can be explored by selecting them from the **Data Feature** dropdown menu. Different climate factors are displayed as **Line Charts** showing their development over time on the right side. The arrangement of the four line charts can be switched to a second linked map component (**Map Detail View**) for a more detailed spatial analysis or a side-by-side comparison. The view provides additional contextual information by enabling the overlay of data on mosquito presence and dengue serotype counts, thus providing a more comprehensive basis for risk assessment.
