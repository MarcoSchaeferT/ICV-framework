### Interactions
Move the cursor over countries and grid cells or click to center to a country or to get details on demand. This view is controlled by the **Overview Map**. This means that all interactions and adjustments made in the **Overview Map** are reflected here. Please refer to the <span style={{ display: "inline-flex", verticalAlign: "middle" }}><OpenInBrowser className="h-4" /></span> icon of the **Overview Map** for more information.

| Action    | How to Use                          |
|-----------|-------------------------------------|
| **Click** | Countries are selectable. A click on country can automatically pan the view to the country center.
| **Hover** | When moving the cursor over a colored grid cells a tooltip appears, providing  detailed information on the current grid cell.

Legends in the bottom left corner show the current scale (*km per length*) and the zoom and coordinates of the center point of the view (*latitude, longitude, zoom*).
