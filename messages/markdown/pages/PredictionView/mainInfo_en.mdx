This view highlights the prediction of mosquito habitat suitability using machine learning models. It consists of two linked views: the **Overview Map** and the **Detail Map** for more granular inspections. The interactive maps visualize the predicted suitability for different mosquito species based on environmental factors and reported sightings. The view provides additional contextual information by enabling the overlay of data on mosquito presence and dengue serotype counts, thus providing a more comprehensive basis for risk assessment. The **Detail Map** allows for a closer examination of specific regions, enabling the exploration of local variations in habitat suitability and presence data in a zoomed view. (data source: <a href={dataSourceURLs.mosquito}>Zenodo</a>)
