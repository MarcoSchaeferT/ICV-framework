### Detail Map

This world map focuses on visualizing grid-based data on the habitat suitability of different mosquito species (see [Vector Information](./domainInfo)). It serves as a *detail map* on which selected areas of the **Overview Map** are shown in a higher zoom level.

---------------

### How To Use
This view has limited interaction possibilities (click, hover). It is mainly controlled by the **Overview Map**. This means that all interactions and adjustments made in the **Overview Map** are reflected here. For more information please refer to the <span style={{ display: "inline-flex", verticalAlign: "middle" }}><InfoIcon size={16} /></span> symbol of the **Overview Map**.
